About the Hackathon

CODE मंथन 2.0 is a 24-hour hackathon organized by AIMIS (Anand Institute of Management and Information Science) and Shri Ramkrishna Seva Mandal (SRKSM), backed by Student Startup Innovation Policy (SSIP) and Institution's Innovation Council (IIC). Build impactful solutions across Hardware and Software tracks.

  • Team size: 2–6 participants
  • On-site mentoring and mini-workshops
  • Judging based on innovation, impact, and execution
What to expect

Build. Learn. Win.

High-energy venue, resources to prototype fast, and a community that loves to create.

Swag Goodies Mentors Prizes Certificate

Competition Categories

Hardware

Prototype with sensors, microcontrollers, robotics, and IoT. Create tangible solutions that interact with the physical world.

  • Electronics, Arduino/RPi, embedded systems
  • 3D printing, fabrication support (if available)

Software

Build web, mobile, and AI-powered apps. Focus on usability, scalability, and real impact.

  • Web, Mobile, Cloud, AI/ML
  • APIs, integrations, data visualizations
